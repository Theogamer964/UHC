Whats UHC?

UHC is a gamemode where you and your team are teleported randomly into the map. ... You are given a set time to prepare, after this time PvP is enabled and the world border starts to slowly shrink forcing you to head to the middle of the map!

Why should you choose us?

Our server supports cracked launchers of minecraft which means you dont have to buy the game to join. We have many plugins that will sure give you an amazing experience of the uhc, if you need/believe we should add something else go to #suggestions channel in our discord server and feel free to tell us what you think! 

How do i take part in a UHC?

After joining our discord server and passed our application, We will send you the ip of the server and whitelist you within 24 hours.We are announcing the uhc 2-3 days before it happen so there is no reason to be anxious. We are planning to make at least 1 UHC in a week and we will be happy to see you there!

Do I win something by winning a uhc?

unfortunately because we do not have many players on the server we can not give something as a reward but if get at least 10-20 players on the discord server we will start giving prizes to the winners of the uhcs
